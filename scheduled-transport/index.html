Scheduled Transport

Wheelchair van, critical care transport, and non-emergency ambulance service.

One call. Local care.

When your residents need the highest level of care and attention for critical care, non-emergency ambulance, and wheelchair transportation, turn to our scheduled transport, from your local non-profit paramedic ambulance service, Rockford Ambulance.

Extending your care beyond your walls

Our scheduled options give your residents the best transportation for:

  • Doctor appointments
  • Hospital visits
  • Dialysis treatments
  • Examinations
  • Other medical appointments
  • Returning from the hospital or special care
  • And almost any other special situation.

Fast, easy scheduling

Whether you need a single transport, want to schedule a series of appointments, or have a last-minute request, scheduled transport from Rockford Ambulance makes it easy. One call to our team puts everything into motion. Let us simplify your scheduling and make your life easier!

Did you know?

We guard your reputation on every trip. You know us because we’re your neighbors and friends. Our kids learn and play together, and we see each other throughout the year. We don’t just serve the community — we’re part of the community.
