Training Locations

Rockford

CPR Classes in “Rockford” are held at our Courtland Ambulance Sub Station which is located at the corner of 14 Mile Rd. (M-57) & Berrigan Ave. NE. American Red Cross Babysitter Training Classes are held here as well.

7490 14 Mile Rd. NE, Rockford MI 49341

Lowell 

CPR Classes in “Lowell” are held at the First United Methodist Church (adjacent white building) located at the corner of East Main & Jackson Street.

519 East Main St, Lowell MI 49331

Sparta

CPR Classes in “Sparta” are held at the Meadowlark Retirement Home located on Ida Red near the intersection of 13 Mile Rd (West Division) & M-37.

65 Ida Red, Sparta MI 49345

Grand Rapids Township

CPR Classes in “Grand Rapids Township” are held at the GR Twp Offices located on East Beltline NE just south of Knapp.

1836 E Beltline Ave NE, Grand Rapids, MI 49525

Grand Rapids (Downtown) 

CPR Classes in “Grand Rapids (Downtown)” are held at T.G.I.Friday’s Restaurant located on Monroe Street just north of Fulton.

50 N. Monroe Ave., NW, Grand Rapids MI 49503

Grand Rapids (NE)

CPR Classes in “Grand Rapids (NE)” are held at Plymouth Christian High School located on Plymouth Street between Michigan & Leonard NE.

965 Plymouth St. NE, Grand Rapids MI 49505

Walker (NW)

CPR Classes in “Walker (NW)” are held at Lincoln Square Retirement located on Lake Michigan Drive ½ way between I-196 & Wilson Avenue.

3121 Lake Michigan Dr., NW Walker, MI 49504

Wyoming (SW)

CPR Classes in “Wyoming (SW)” are held at Aurora Pond Retirement located on Byron Center Road north of the METRO Health Hospital (M-6) SW.

2380 Aurora Pond Dr. SW, Wyoming, MI 49315

Kentwood (SE)

CPR Classes in “Kentwood (SE)” are held at American Dive Zone located on Broadmoor (East Beltline) south of 28th Street SE.

3089 Broadmoor Ave. SE, Kentwood MI 49512

Grattan - Belding

CPR Classes in “Grattan - Belding” are held at the Grattan Township Fire Department located west of Lincoln Lake on Old Belding Road next to the Township offices.

12134 Old Belding Road, Belding, MI 48809

Newago

CPR Classes in “Newaygo” are held at River Valley Community Church located on 88th Street near M-37.

53 East 88th Street, Newaygo MI 49337
