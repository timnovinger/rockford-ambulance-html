Emergency Transport

Local Professionals. World-class care.

When you see an ambulance, you think of us. Since 1968, our non-profit paramedic service watched over our communities night and day, and we proudly keep doing it today. With stations located in Rockford, Lowell, Sparta, Belmont and Grand Rapids Charter Township, we respond to your emergency within moments. 

Always right around the corner.

We are dedicated to providing the best pre-hospital services for the residents of the communities we serve.  We have a very caring and compassionate staff of highly skilled Paramedics, Specialists and EMT-Basics ready to serve your emergency and on-going medical needs. You know us because we're your neighbors and friends. Our kids play together, and we see each other throughout the year. We don't just serve the community -- we're part of the community.
