Employment

Rockford Ambulance is looking for highly motivated individuals who are committed to providing high quality, professional and compassionate medical services to our customers.

Rockford Ambulance offers:

  • Competitive annual salaries
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Newer fleet of well equipped vehicles
  • Stations with amenities for your comfort
  • Medical Insurance
  • Dental Insurance
  • Annual performance reviews with increases
  • Paid time off
  • Holiday pay
  • 403 (b) retirement plan
  • Continuing education
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Employee assistance program

If you are interested in becoming part of the Rockford Ambulance team, please contact Matt McConnon in our Rockford location in order to discuss available opportunities. We look forward to hearing from you.
